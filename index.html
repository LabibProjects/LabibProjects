About Me.

I am L.M. Mahir Labib, a 16-year-old tech innovator from Bangladesh, passionate about leveraging technology to create meaningful impact. As the Co-founder & CTO of UddharBD, I’ve developed a platform to assist flood victims by connecting them with rescue teams, volunteers, and verified donation sources. I’m also leading Agrisense, a project focused on revolutionizing agriculture by eliminating middlemen, ensuring farmers and consumers benefit directly from the produce trade. In addition, I’m working on Rokto-Sondhan, a blood donation platform designed to connect donors with those in need, ensuring life-saving support reaches people on time. My work also extends to AI research, where I recently contributed to a paper on the ethical implications and biases in machine learning for finance in South Asia. Currently, I’m involved in developing the first Bangla AI voicebot, a project funded by the World Bank. This project aims to make technology accessible to Bangla-speaking populations, bringing AI-driven solutions to everyday tasks. With expertise in programming (Python, C, C++), web development, and UI/UX design, I’m always pushing boundaries to innovate.
